J2EE with 'Conversations' overview:
Having a client platform for service providers which can be installed locally and logged in to via secure connection
would allow the distribution of a protal through which to interact with customers.
Customers could interact with the service through either a distributed platform, or a more preferable option
would be for customers to access the servive through a web browser with secure login. 
Information regarding client and customer would be accessed through J2EE servers.
